About 1521kohi

Our Vision

To offer premium quality drinks at a price everyone can enjoy.

Our Mission

To serve high-quality drinks while ensuring affordability and accessibility for all our customers.

Our Story

The name '1521kohi' was inspired by our location's address. We're a Gen Z themed café, reflected in our creative menu names and vibrant atmosphere.

Our Services

Operating Hours:

Open daily from 2:00 PM to 10:00 PM
Last call for orders is at 9:30 PM.

Pre-Orders:

Pre-orders placed before opening time are accepted.
Deliveries begin at 2:00 PM.

Service Options:

  • Pick-up
  • Dine-in
  • Delivery

Delivery Areas:

We deliver within Golden City, S.R.L., including:
Phase 1, Phase 2, Phase 2E Mabuhay, Phase 3, Phase 4, and Phase 4E.

Outside Golden City:

Deliveries to areas beyond Golden City are booked via Angkas or Joyride, with the delivery fee (DF) to be shouldered by the buyer or receiver.

Meet-Up Option:

Meet-ups at Jollibee Golden are also accepted.

Delivery Fees (Within Golden City, Dila, S.R.L.):

  • ₱40 for 2 drinks
  • ₱20 for 3 drinks
  • Free delivery for 4 or more drinks
